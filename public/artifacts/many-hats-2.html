The World Once Asked You to Choose

Designer or developer? Artist or engineer? Creative or coder?

For decades, careers came with a lane. You picked one and stayed in it.

Creative engineers refuse to pick just one.

They wear the inventor's cap with the artist's beret, the coder's hood with the storyteller's fedora.
